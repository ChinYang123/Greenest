As more people move into apartments and high-rises, access to nature shrinks. Urban gardening closes that gap.

## The benefits

- **Fresher food** with zero food miles.
- **Cooler buildings** — green walls and roofs cut heat.
- **Better wellbeing** — tending plants lowers stress.

GreenNest exists to make this accessible to everyone, regardless of how small your space is.
