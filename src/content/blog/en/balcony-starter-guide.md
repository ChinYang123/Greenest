Starting a balcony garden is simpler than it looks. Here is a weekend plan.

## Saturday — Plan & assemble

1. **Measure your space.** Note width, depth, and which direction it faces (sun matters most).
2. **Assemble your vertical planter.** Tiered planters make the most of a small footprint.
3. **Fill with organic soil.** Leave 2cm below the rim for watering.

## Sunday — Plant & water

1. **Place sun-lovers high, shade-tolerant plants low.**
2. **Water deeply once**, then let the self-watering reservoir take over.
3. **Label everything** so you remember what is growing where.

That's it — in one weekend you have a working garden. Use our [Design Your Garden](/en/design-your-garden) tool to pick the right plants for your light.
