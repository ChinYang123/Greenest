Most beginners love their plants to death with too much water. Here's a reliable routine.

## The finger test

Push a finger 2cm into the soil. **Dry? Water. Damp? Wait.** That single habit prevents most root rot.

## Match water to the plant

- **Low-water:** succulents, snake plant, rosemary — every 7–10 days.
- **Medium:** basil, lettuce, marigold — every 2–3 days.
- **High:** tomato, fern, mint — daily in hot weather.

A self-watering reservoir smooths out the busy days so your plants never swing between drought and flood.
